About Seoul
Seoul, the capital of the Republic of Korea, is a metropolis with modern high-rise buildings, high-tech subways,
popular culture and temples, old palaces and street vendors.
Notable attractions include the Dongdaemun Design Plaza,
a convention hall with ultra-modern design featuring curved exterior and rooftop park,
Gyeongbok Palace which once had 7,000 rooms, Jogye Temple with painting trees and pine trees.
